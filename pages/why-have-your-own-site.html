Why Have Your Own Website

Bento
writes on August 20,2020

Having your own website is a powerful marketing tool. Developing your web site offers many benefits as shall be pointed below, todays average person searches online about a service or product before they go out looking in the real world. A well-maintained website can help you gain a competitive advantage in your industry and improve your customer service.

1. Easy Access to New Customers
You can have your existing customers refer you to their friends and relatives using only your web address or URL.

2. Educate Your Customers
Your website can offer free advice about your products and services. This information can be delivered in a well thought out and consistent way any time of the day or night.

3. Improve Your Advertising Effectiveness
Provide photos and detailed descriptions of your products or services. Explain why your products or services are superior to your competitors. Show visitors how your products or services can help them in their personal or professional lives.

4. Tell People Who You Are What You Do.
Your site allows customers to find out more about you, your product, and gather any information they need. Even if it is just the peace of mind that you have an online presence, this lets a potential customer start to take you seriously. You are building greater trust with the more information you are providing.

5. Looks and is professional
With all the benefits having a site brings to a business, the importance of having a website should be clear to see.We could sum it up and say well everyone has a website these days, but that doesn’t tell half the story. If you want to be taken seriously as a business, then you need to be serious about taking business. Nothing says professional business more than your own professional site. Nothing connects more with your customers online.

6. Enhance Your Online Presence
Of the 7.4 Billion people in this world, 3.5 Billion people or 47% are online every day.They are searching for information, sharing on social media or shopping on e-commerce websites.This number is expected to grow as the world becomes increasingly dependent on mobile technology to search the Internet. If your small business does not have a website, you will be undermining its ability to generate income. And not just any website; but a mobile responsive website, one that can be accessed by mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. Statistically, 65% of online traffic comes from mobile devices.

7. Professionalize Your Brand
Many small business owners make the mistake of not working on their brand until they gain traction. You have to start working on your brand before launching your business. The brand gives your business its identity and makes it easier for customers and clients to understand what it is all about. The website is the most effective and efficient way to build and professionalize your brand:

  1. It places your branded value proposition front and center in the Home page.
  2. Website optimization techniques can help Internet users find your brand.
  3. It can be distributed seamlessly through various online channels.

12. Provide Contingencies for Your Brick-and-Mortar Business
If there’s anything that can be learned from the Covid-19 global pandemic is the importance of having a website for your brick and mortar business. The massive spread of Covid-19 necessitated governments worldwide to impose community lockdowns in their respective countries. Businesses came to a standstill – except those that have websites. Businesses with a website were able to run a telecommuting program whereby employees work from home. An employee can log into a built-in portal where they can sign- in, retrieve the day’s assignments, and upload completed work.
